About Bank Index

Bank Index is a national private commercial bank (BUSN) which was established in Jakarta on July 30, 1992. Until now, Bank Index has 50 office networks, all of which are connected online and spread across a number of regions. In addition to developing office networks, Bank Index also continuously develops Information Systems and Technology (TSI) with the main focus on electronic delivery channels.

About Customer Care

Customer care is all efforts made by the company to meet customer needs and satisfaction, as well as maintain good relationships with customers.
